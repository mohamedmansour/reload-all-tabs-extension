Browser Action

To remove the button, right click on the icon and choose Hide button.

To show that the button again, visit the chrome://extensions/ page, and click on Show button.

Context Menu (Right Click)

When you have multiple checkboxes checked, it will place all context menus under one parent "Reload All Tabs" menu.

When you have just a single context menu selected, there will be no parent.
